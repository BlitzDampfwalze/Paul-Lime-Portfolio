Plan-it

This app was built to streamline group travel planning after my own firsthand experience with less than organized individuals. It enables users to create virtual unique rooms to coordinate the planning of a trip. Tasks can be assigned to the various users that have joined the room and modify the schedule for all to see as the tasks of researching various potential activities actualize into concrete plans.

Technologies Utilized:

React.js | Redux | HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript ES6

Recipe Vault

Despite numerous online recipes and sources, they never seem to quite meet our expectations of our Moms' and Grandmas' recipes. The Recipe Vault was an idea inspired in the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey. Tradition and food was one of the last things on our minds at the time. However, no holiday seems quite the same without those traditional family dishes that have been passed down for generations. With the Recipe Vault, users are able to maintain privacy as well as preserve their families' recipes.

Technologies Utilized:

JavaScript ES6 | HTML5 | CSS3 | jQuery
Node.js | Express.js | Mocha | Supertest | RESTful API Endpoints | MongoDB | Mongoose | bcrypt.js | JWT
Github Repo
Live Demo

About Paul

With my professional background in finance and business, I bring a holistic approach to solving problems and developing solutions. I always embrace a challenge and enjoy solving problems effectively. Since I've also always had a passion for technology and engineering, I decided to turn my interests and passions into a new career by immersing myself in the studies of web development.

Over the past six months, I have worked with senior developers several times a week as I built three core projects while developing my knowledge of several technologies from the fundamentals of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to that of Node, React, and Redux to build a full stack project.

Nothing has felt more satisfying than than engaging in my work and constantly solving problems, both new and old. I would be grateful for the opportunity to employ my skills in the industry where I can make both a social and financial impact.

While I still enjoy making a good investment, I was finding the typical day to day corporate finance job mindless and boring. The same cannot be said of software development field, which is constantly evolving.

On a less serious note, I also enjoys many other hobbies when I find any free time in my schedule, such as playing the piano, playing the steirische harmonika, participating in the local community, and engaging in strategic challenges from board games to business negotiations.

My most recent community involvement includes helping with Hurricane Harvey disaster relief and other local non-profit organizations.
