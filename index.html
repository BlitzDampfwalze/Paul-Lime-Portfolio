Plan-it

This app was built to streamline group travel planning after my own firsthand experience with less than organized individuals. It enables uses to create unique rooms to coordinate the planning of a trip. Tasks can be assigned to the various users that have joined the room and modifty the schedule for all to see as the tasks of researching varioius potential activities actualize into concrete plans.

Recipe Vault

Despite numerous online recipes and sources, they never seem to quite meet our expectations of our Moms' and Grandmas' recipes. The Recipe Vault was an idea inspired in the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey. Tradition and food was one of the last things on our minds at the time. However, no holiday seems quite the same without those traditional family dishes that have been passed down for generations. With the Recipe Vault, users are able to maintain privacy as well as preserve their families' recipes.

Technologies

Front-End: HTML5 | CSS3 | JavaScript ES6 | jQuery
Back-End: Node.js | Express.js | Mocha | Supertest | RESTful API Endpoints | MongoDB | Mongoose
Security: bcrypt.js, JWT
